Openreach

For the last eight years I was working for a telecoms company called Openreach. It was an enjoyable place to work and only have good things to say about it, but after eight good years it felt like it was time for a change. The company had been offering voluntary redundancies for team members and I felt that it was my time to move on. I have learned a lot from the company and definitely have skills that will stay with me for a long time. Over the years I have done a few different roles, from starting as an apprentice to leaving as a 'Field Based Coordinator.' As well as many valuable skills, I will be leaving having made many friends that I hope to continue to see outside of work.
